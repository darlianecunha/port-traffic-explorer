European Port Traffic Explorer

Interactive view of vessel arrivals at 15 major European ports, by ship type and size class, from official Eurostat maritime statistics. Select ports, pick a ship type and compare fleet profiles: who receives the container mega-ships, who lives off tankers, and how traffic moved since 2019.
